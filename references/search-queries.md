## Query Best Practices

1. **Always use date filters** — limit to last 24-48 hours for daily briefing
2. **Run 2-3 focus queries first** — Codex/Claude Code/Gemini content is priority
3. **Mix Chinese and English queries** — ensures coverage of both regions
4. **Run 6-8 queries total** — balance between coverage and efficiency
5. **Deduplicate after gathering** — same story will appear in multiple search results
